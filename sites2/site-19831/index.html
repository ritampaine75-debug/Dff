What is Life?

Life is a self-sustaining chemical system capable of Darwinian evolution. It is distinguished from inorganic matter by its capacity for metabolism, growth, response to stimuli, and reproduction.

In biological terms, life exists where entropy is locally reversed, creating complex structures that maintain a state far from thermodynamic equilibrium.
